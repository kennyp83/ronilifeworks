Where Training and Community Outreach Begin

Inclusive Trauma-Informed Care & Community Outreach

Roni LifeWorks in Seattle, Washington provides trauma-informed care training and supportive outreach services that strengthen communities and increase through a cultural lens.

What we offer

All our programs are tailored for all audiences, but are especially focused on the lived experiences of Black, Indigenous and People of Color (BIPOC).

Explore Services

Community Outreach & Training Programs

Youth Outreach

Outreach team connects with unhoused youth to build trust and provide essential resources for housing, healthcare, and safety.

Adult Outreach

Mobile outreach brings food, water, and housing support directly to unhoused adults seeking stability and permanent solutions.

Outreach Pantry

Our Pioneer Square pantry offers food, furniture, and household goods for recently housed individuals and families.

Our Mission

To empower individuals and communities through education, skills development, and access to resources, fostering self-sufficiency and positive change.

Our Vision

A community where everyone has the opportunity to thrive, with a skilled workforce and a vibrant, inclusive environment.
