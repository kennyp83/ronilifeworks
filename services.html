Trauma-Informed Care Trainings

We travel to organizations to assist them in creating customized training that accommodates staff and community members working outside traditional business hours.

Training Topics:

  • Case Management 101 & 102 (Certification)
  • WA State Domestic Violence 101 (20 hrs)
  • Health & Wellness in the Black and Brown Community
  • Trauma-Informed Care (People of Color Lens)
  • Rape Trauma & Women of Color
  • Transgenerational Trauma in the Black Community
  • At-Risk Youth of Color
  • Safety Planning (4 hrs)
  • Legal Advocacy 101 (4 hrs)
  • Advanced Legal Advocacy 102 (8 hrs, Cert)
  • Cultural Competency 101 & 102 (4 hrs each)
  • Let’s Celebrate Differences! (Children Series)
  • Impact of Domestic Violence (Children Series)
  • Diversity, Inclusion, Equity & Belonging (4 & 8 hrs)

Specialized Supportive Outreach Services

Monthly Breaking Cycles, Healing Community Circle Groups

Breaking Cycles, Healing Community is a sacred and empowering healing circle support group that serves as an inspiration of strength and resilience within the community for domestic violence. This space is where cultural traditions and supportive community members embody a spirit of unity and hope.

Supportive Outreach & Coaching

Health & Wellness Empowerment Coaching

We help clients re-establish safety, build empowerment skills, process experiences, manage emotions, and collaborate with care teams. Sessions cover domestic violence, parenting, boundaries, healthy relationships, red flags, coping skills, communication, and more (8 to 16 sessions).

Employment Coaching

Our employment coaching program provides resume prep, interview skills, conflict resolution, job search skills, financial planning, and long-term career coaching to promote financial stability.

EMBRACE Coaching

Our EMBRACE coaching helps employees and leadership create inclusive workplaces through unconscious bias training, conflict management, DEI, equity, belonging, and cultural awareness practices.

Health & Life Coaching

Our 8-16 session Health & Life coaching supports lasting personal change, helping clients build strategies for long-term personal wellness and self-directed growth.

Reiki Healing Services

We offer specialized Reiki services for individuals seeking holistic mind-body healing as part of their self-care and recovery journey.
